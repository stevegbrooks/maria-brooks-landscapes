Welcome to Maria Brooks Landscapes!

Since 1998 we have been designing, installing and maintaining extraordinary gardens on the Main Line.

We can develop a plan for your garden that incorporates garden beds, containers, hardscaping, irrigation and lighting infrastructure, along with phasing of work to allow the design to unfold over multiple seasons.

Above all, we are responsive to our customers. Our designs reflect their personal preferences and aspirations, and we view developing and maintaining of each landscape as a continuing partnership between ourselves and our customers.

Services we provide:

  • Garden design and installation
  • Complete plan of area with plant lists
  • Pots and planters for patios, pedestals, and entrances
  • Automated irrigation system design and installation
  • Automated low-voltage lighting system design and installation
  • Seasonal or monthly maintenance programs
