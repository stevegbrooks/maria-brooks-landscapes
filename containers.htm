Containers

A well-placed container will create a great first impression, a focal point for the garden, and bring color and texture to a patio, deck or path.

Seasonal changes with containers can transform a space multiple times a year. We do containers for residences, office buildings and restaurants, and can provide automatic watering, or connect them to existing irrigation systems.

And we offer maintenance services, or can provide detailed instructions for taking care of the plants yourself.
