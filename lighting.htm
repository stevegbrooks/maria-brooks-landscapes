Lighting

Enjoy your landscape and home investment during the quiet and leisure time of evening with a landscape lighting system. Nothing makes your home look more beautiful and “upscale” than a professionally installed landscape lighting system.

By installing landscape lighting, your home and your plantings remain beautiful after dark as well as in the daylight hours. The low voltage systems provide for low-cost operation and installation is surprisingly affordable.

Maria Brooks Landscapes provides a complete landscape lighting service: Design, Installation and Maintenance. We will meet with you at your property and lay out a custom-designed lighting system that accentuates the specific attributes of your home and landscaping, so your home will stand out at night in a unique way.
