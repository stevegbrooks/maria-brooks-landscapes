Gardens

Our projects can be as small as replanting an existing perennial bed or new installation for a large estate. We do perennial borders, courtyards, intimate patio borders, large perimeter beds, foundation plantings for new construction, deer-proof gardens and everything in between.

Right Plant, Right Place

The idea of finding the right plant for the right place has become a truism in landscape design, but we take it seriously. Here are some of the things we consider in planning a garden:
  • Your expectations and preferences
  • Deer resistance
  • Sun exposure
  • Soil type and drainage
  • Water conditions
  • Attraction of butterflies and birds
  • Plant height, shape, texture, color
  • Seasonal interest

The Process

Although many projects involve only soome of these steps, the overall process starts with a site assessment, followed by design, including positioning and extent of law, beds and hardscaping; terrain preparation; soil amendmnet; planting; irrigation and lighting; mulching; and cleanup.

Support

If gardens are to reach their potential, they need regular attention and maintenance. After completion of a project, we can provide an illustrated Plant Portfolio, including a fertilizing and maintenance guide, to provide pointers for when and how to maintain the plantings. On request, we can develop a maintenance program and provide the service. Typically, we do cleanup, pruning, and fertilizing in early spring; trimming and weeding through the summer; and cleanup and winter preparation in the fall.
